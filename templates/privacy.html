Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Memoraid (“we”, “our”, or “us”) collects, uses, and protects your personal data when you use our services.

1. Information We Collect

  • Your email and password when you register an account
  • Newsletter content and form entries you submit
  • Page visit data and newsletter open rates

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Generate and schedule newsletter content
  • Deliver emails via SendGrid
  • Track usage data to improve user experience
  • Send promotional communications (you can unsubscribe anytime)

3. AI Content & Third Parties

We use OpenAI to generate newsletter content. No personal data is shared with OpenAI. We share user email data with SendGrid solely for the purpose of delivering emails.

4. Data Retention & Security

Your data is stored indefinitely unless you delete your account. Passwords are securely hashed. You may delete your account and all associated data at any time from the dashboard.
